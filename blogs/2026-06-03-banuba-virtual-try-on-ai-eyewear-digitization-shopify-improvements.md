Banuba released an updated TINT platform featuring three major enhancements for its Shopify virtual try-on plugin: AI-powered eyewear digitization that requires only one or two photos to generate complete 3D models, an improved onboarding experience designed for non-technical users, and support for colored contact lens try-on. These additions bring the total number of supported product categories to six.
